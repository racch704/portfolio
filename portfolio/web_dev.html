Who?

Glentruim Homestay B&B and Ecopods is a family owned hospitality business. They offer two self catering Ecopods and three rooms in their homestay bed and breakfast. They pride themselves on welcoming guest from all over the world to their estate.

Why?

The business owners currently host their holiday let accomodation on a range of third party websites. however, these sites take a large commission on bookings. The owners would like to have their own website to promote their business and take direct bookings.
